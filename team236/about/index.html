About Team 236

Team 236, the TechnoTicks, is a FIRST Robotics team based out of Lyme-Old Lyme High School in Connecticut. The team was founded in 1999 by Mr. Derry and a group of dedicated students. After the team was established and continued to have success in the region, we moved forward. Thanks to community outreach and support of the Connecticut FLL community, among other work, the TechnoTicks won the Chairman's Award at the national level in 2009. Since then, the team has continued to promote science and technology and to improve in robot design. To date, the team has won 16 blue banners in its 17 years of competition.
